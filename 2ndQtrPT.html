Ways to recover endangered and threatened species
1. Learn About the species and the dangers they face.

The number one reason why animals like the Visayan Spotted Deer are being near extinction or endangered are because of predators, we should know about these predators and protect these animals by reporting it.

2. Never purchase products made from endangered or threatened species.

Buying products that are made from endangered or threatened species, motivates the people who are making the product to make even more of them, causing the extinction of ones kind.

3. Visiting National parks, Wildlife refuges or protected areas.

These areas are full of threatened species, by visiting these areas, you are helping the protected area become even more protected, as the government will allocate with protecting these areas.
